WordCamp San Francisco

About WordCamp

The beautiful city of San Francisco, CA will be the host city for WordCamp!

Explore the future of WordPress with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover new techniques to advance your career as a web designer.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international WordPress community and share skills with some of the world's top experts and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of WordPress.

WordCamp is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

WordPress 5: New Gutenberg editor WooCommerce: E-commerce platform Hosting: Managed web hosting Themes: Creating your theme Plugins: Develop your plugin Content: Writing and podcasts

Speakers

Jill Palmer

Recruited by two of the nation’s leading IT consulting firms to provide project management over large-scale, top-priority and complex technology initiatives, Jill has been a driving force in the tech industry for years.

Derek Wilson

An innovator of next-generation solutions, systems and applications; Derek has given companies he has worked with a competitive edge and produced outstanding results for customers.

Julian Stewart

Delivered industry-leading software that saved clients millions of dollars, shortened processes from weeks to minutes, captured #1 market share and generated up to $21M in annual revenues within year one of launch.

Rebecca Mori

A trendsetter in moving between agile and waterfall approaches depending on project specifics and client goals. Rebecca is most strategic when designing detailed project road maps and work breakdown structures.

Abilene Parkens

Has created over 21 fortune 500 company websites, as well as many B2C sites and several B2B site across multiple channels while streamlining functions by developing and implementing a task management ticket system.

Mark Lether

Led design and multinational rollout of robust, scalable and secure electronic data interchange (EDI), enterprise resource planning (ERP) and point-of-sale (POS) systems for clients including AmEx, Citigroup and McDonald’s.
